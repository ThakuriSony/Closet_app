OUTFIT GENERATION LOGIC (VERY IMPORTANT):

We are EXTENDING the existing outfit engine.

Update function:
generateOutfit(clothingItems, occasion, weather)

---

STEP 1 — WEATHER INTERPRETATION

Convert temperature into category:

- <10°C → Cold
- 10–20°C → Mild
- >20°C → Hot

---

STEP 2 — BASE REQUIREMENTS

Always try to include:
- 1 Top
- 1 Bottom
- 1 Shoes

---

STEP 3 — WEATHER RULES

Cold:
- MUST include Outerwear (if available)
- Prefer thicker fabrics (tags: jacket, hoodie, wool)

Mild:
- Outerwear optional

Hot:
- Avoid outerwear
- Prefer light tags (cotton, breathable, shorts)

---

STEP 4 — OCCASION RULES

Work:
- Prefer clean/simple tags (formal, neutral)
- Avoid gym/sporty items

Casual:
- Flexible, allow most items

Gym:
- MUST include sporty/active tags

Event:
- Prefer stylish/dark/bold tags

---

STEP 5 — FILTER + SCORE SYSTEM (IMPORTANT)

Instead of random selection:

- Score each clothing item:
  +1 if matches occasion
  +1 if matches weather
  +1 if frequently used tag match

- Pick highest scoring items per category

---

STEP 6 — FALLBACK

If no perfect match:
- Relax filters gradually
- Always return a usable outfit

---

STEP 7 — OUTPUT

Return:
- Top
- Bottom
- Shoes
- Optional Outerwear
