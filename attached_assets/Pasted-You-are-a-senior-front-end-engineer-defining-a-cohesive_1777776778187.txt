for the Wearwise app.

GOAL:
Adopt a soft, eye-friendly color palette using:
- Soft Blue as the PRIMARY action color
- Soft Yellow as a SECONDARY surface color

PRIMARY COLOR:
#4282AA  (Soft Blue)

SECONDARY COLOR:
#FFEDA2  (Soft Yellow)

IMPORTANT:
This palette must feel calm, editorial, and user-led.
Do NOT use harsh or high-contrast color blocking.

- Background: #4282AA

Keep existing:
- Off-white / cream canvas (#FAF7F0)
- Grid lines

------------------------------------------------------------
4) TABS & SELECTION STATES

Selected occasion tab:
- Background: #4282AA
- Text: white

Unselected tabs:
- Neutral background and text
- No blue/yellow tint

5) CONSISTENCY CHECK

After update:
